Residential Collection

Get responsible waste disposal and removal services, we Swachha offers residential solid waste service offering Residential Waste Removal: We offer responsible door to door waste collection services for households in partnership in Urban Local Bodies.

Transportation of Waste

Waste transportation services help ensure companies follow government regulations for waste disposal. To stay in compliance and develop a cost-effective waste development solution, many businesses choose to work with Swachha as we have in-house fleet of vehicles.

Aggregation Facility

We have Aggregating facilitate infra required for material segregation, storage, size reduction which will help in creating value addition for the waste and achieve maximum recovery of the waste.

Recycling of Waste

We have Dry Waste Processing Centre where tertiary segregated waste is transported to our recycling facility for converting waste into useful finished products

Composting

We have a decentralised wet waste composting facility ,source segregated organics which are collected from Door-Door /Bulk Generators will transported in a separate vehicles and composted into useful manure

Safe disposal

The non-recyclable waste such as Resource Derived Fuel (RDF) /Multi-Layer Plastic (MLP)/Low Value Plastic (LVP) is converted into REPOLYMIX – Grades which are used as an alternative fuel to Cement, Steel Industries and also used in Road Construction.

Municipality/Urban Local Bodies

Swachha Eco Solutions has a vast experience in working with the Urban Local Bodies /Corporations in provides collection services including street sweeping, doorstep pickup and transportation in the most efficient way all the way to the processing centre, including the use of transfer stations where necessary.We partner with municipalities for the reliable collection of waste to keep cities & towns clean and for sustainability of the planet.

Extended Producers Responsibilty

Swachha is also an Extended Producers Responsibility Organisation that helps producers meet their EPR targets through various recyclers and dismantling process. We have skilled experts working on an end-to-end model that we designed ourselves for managing and processing plastic waste produced across the country.Our experts provide assistance right from EPR registration, certification and authorisation to completely saving the environment from Plastic Waste.

Plastic Waste Recycling

Registration/Implementation

Process starts with registration at CPCB/SPCB by submission of relevant documents required by CPCB which mainly consist of an action plan, agreement, certificates, licenses, etc. Next step starts with the role of authorised waste management agency to collect back agreed volume of post-consumer plastic waste in a period of 1 year from desired location and recycle or dispose it safely as per CPCB/SPCB guidelines.

Collection & Disposal

Swachha has the large network of Collection & Disposals of MLP & Non MLP Plastic Waste from across the country. We work with Municipal Corporation of every state where plastic waste is generated in bulk quantity and having Dry Waste Collection Centres across India. Swachha has a logistics company with an own fleet of trucks for collection and transportation of plastic waste from anywhere in India.

Methods of Waste Disposal under EPR

Waste collected under the EPR is transported to the nearest disposal or recycling facility and processed through one of the following methods

1. Used as fuel in Cement Kiln

2. WTE Recovery

3. Recycling

Can We Recycle Multi-Layered Plastic?

Yes, The Swachha Eco Solutions recycles Multi-layered Postconsumer Plastic Waste into ROPOLYMIX further which can be used to Road Construction and Cement/Steel Manufacturing Units as an Alternative Fuel.
